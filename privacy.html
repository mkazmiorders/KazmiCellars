Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Kazmi Cellars collects, uses, and protects any information when you visit kazmicellars.com. We believe in transparency and keeping things simple.

1. Information We Collect

Kazmi Cellars is primarily a read-only content site. We collect minimal personal information. The only personal information we collect is what you voluntarily provide when you contact us directly via email. We do not require account registration, and you can browse the entire Site without providing any personal information.

2. Contact Information

If you contact us via email at kazmicellars@gmail.com, we may retain your email address and the content of your message in order to respond to your inquiry. We will not share this information with third parties, and we will not add you to any mailing list without your explicit consent.

3. Newsletter

When The Kazmi Letter newsletter launches, subscribers will be asked to provide their email address voluntarily. You can unsubscribe at any time. We will never sell, rent, or share subscriber email addresses with third parties. Your email is used solely to deliver the newsletter.

4. Cookies and Analytics

Kazmi Cellars may use basic analytics tools to understand how visitors use the Site — such as which pages are most visited and where traffic comes from. This data is aggregated and anonymous, and is used solely to improve the Site experience. We do not use cookies to track individual users across the web or for advertising purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

This Site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages and uses GitHub for content management. These services may collect basic technical information such as IP addresses and browser types as part of their standard operations. Please refer to Cloudflare's and GitHub's respective privacy policies for more information on how they handle data.

6. Affiliate Links

Some links on this Site may be affiliate links. When you click these links, the affiliated retailer may set cookies on your device as part of their tracking systems. Kazmi Cellars has no control over these cookies. Clicking affiliate links is entirely optional.

7. Children's Privacy

This Site contains content related to alcohol and is intended for adults of legal drinking age only. Kazmi Cellars does not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you are under the legal drinking age in your jurisdiction, please do not use this Site.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect any information you share with us. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of any information transmitted to or from this Site.

9. Your Rights

If you have provided us with personal information and wish to have it deleted or corrected, please contact us at kazmicellars@gmail.com and we will promptly accommodate your request.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of the Site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at kazmicellars@gmail.com.
